Ricotta Cheesecake Recipe

Description

Ricotta cheesecake is a delightful variation of the classic cheesecake, where creamy ricotta cheese replaces some or all of the traditional cream cheese. This Italian-inspired dessert offers a lighter texture and a slightly tangy flavor profile compared to its cream cheese counterpart. Combined with eggs, sugar, and often a hint of vanilla or citrus zest, ricotta cheesecake bakes into a smooth and velvety filling with a subtle richness. It's typically baked on a buttery crust, adding a delightful contrast in texture. Served chilled, topped with fresh fruit, compote, or a dusting of powdered sugar, ricotta cheesecake is a luscious and elegant dessert that beautifully showcases the creamy goodness of ricotta cheese.

Ingredients

For the crust
  • Crushed Biscotti
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Unsalted Butter, Melted
For the filling
  • Ricotta Cheese, well-drained
  • Granulated Sugar
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Lemon Zest
  • All-Purpose Flour
  • Pinch of Salt
