Pasta di Mandorla

Description

Pasta di mandorla, also known as almond paste or almond dough, is a traditional Italian sweet treat made primarily from ground almonds, sugar, and sometimes egg whites. It has a rich, marzipan-like texture and a distinct almond flavor. Pasta di mandorla is commonly used in various desserts and pastries, such as cookies, cakes, and tarts. It can be shaped into intricate designs or simply rolled into small balls and dusted with powdered sugar. The versatility of pasta di mandorla makes it a popular ingredient in Italian confectionery, adding both flavor and visual appeal to a wide range of sweets.

Ingredients

  • Almonds
  • Sugar
  • Egg Whites
  • Almond Extract
  • Lemon Zest
  • Vanilla Extract
